# Free cleans for new parents — Forest Hall

> Programme design for **Tyneside Charity**. Published on [tyneside.charity](https://tyneside.charity).

## Summary

We’re starting small and scaling fast in the **Forest Hall** postcode (**approx. 400 births per year**).

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Package** | 2-hour deep clean (“welcome home”) |
| **Cost per clean** | **£30** (£15 per hour to the cleaner) |
| **Full postcode annual cost** | **£12,000 / year** (400 × £30) |
| **Funding** | **100% funded by the charity** in every phase |

Early phases are **self-fundable from Michael’s personal money**; donations and gifts cover scale once public.

This is the model discussed with **Julie Marriott** (Family Gateway): hyper-local proof, then postcode by postcode — real jobs and real support for new families in the North East.

## Funding phases

All phases are **100% funded by the charity**.

| Phase | Additional cleans | Cost this phase | Cumulative cleans | Cumulative cost |
|--------|-------------------:|----------------:|------------------:|----------------:|
| Pilot (launch) | 10 | £300 | 10 | £300 |
| First expansion | +50 | £1,500 | 60 | £1,800 |
| Second expansion | +100 | £3,000 | 160 | £4,800 |
| Third expansion | +200 | £6,000 | 360 | £10,800 |
| Full postcode | +40 (to reach 400) | £1,200 | 400 | £12,000 / year |

**Total annual cost once fully scaled in Forest Hall = £12,000.**

## Extend or gift cleans

| Who | How | Money |
|-----|-----|--------|
| **Parents** | Pay to **extend** any clean | **£15 per hour**, minimum **2 hours** |
| **Relatives / friends** | **Gift** extra cleans (baby showers, welcome-home presents) | Same rate |

**100% of every paid pound goes straight to the cleaner.**

## Scaling logic

1. Prove the model in **Forest Hall**.  
2. Create paid cleaner hours and software usage in the wild.  
3. Expand **postcode by postcode** using the same unit economics (£30 / clean).

## Why this exists (founder’s note)

Michael’s ambition is to bring **prosperity to the North East** in the highest-leverage personal way he can find. Not a one-off donation — money and effort that **multiply**.

### Personal experience

In rough periods, keeping a house clean can slip — and that can strain family relationships. Hiring a cleaner was one of the **highest-impact supports** he has received: practical, dignifying, and out of proportion to how “small” it sounds.

The moment that crystallised the programme was the weeks after his daughter **Harriet** was born: visitors, sleepless nights, no rest. The gift he wants for new parents is **two hours** back — time to relax and be with the baby.

### Ripple effects

| Ripple | Intent |
|--------|--------|
| **Parents** | Free 2-hour welcome-home deep clean |
| **Cleaners** | Paid work and a pool of new customers (companies started to support this) |
| **Software** | Real-world field-services product (Michael’s professional craft) |
| **Region** | Jobs + capability that compound beyond a single gift |
