# Free cleans for new parents — Howden Ward

> Programme design for **Tyneside Charity**. Based at **Howden Community Hub**.  
> Published on [tyneside.charity](https://tyneside.charity).

## Summary

First area: **Howden Ward**, North Tyneside (not a full postcode — a ward of roughly **12–13k** people).

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Package** | 2-hour deep clean (“welcome home”) |
| **Cost per clean** | **£30** (£15 per hour to the cleaner) |
| **Births / year (est.)** | **~150** (planning figure) |
| **Full-ward annual cost** | **~£4,500 / year** (150 × £30) |
| **Funding** | **100% funded by the charity** in every phase |
| **Base** | Howden Community Hub |

## Funding phases

| Phase | Additional cleans | Cost this phase | Cumulative cleans | Cumulative cost |
|--------|-------------------:|----------------:|------------------:|----------------:|
| Pilot (launch) | 10 | £300 | 10 | £300 |
| First expansion | +30 | £900 | 40 | £1,200 |
| Second expansion | +50 | £1,500 | 90 | £2,700 |
| Third expansion | +40 | £1,200 | 130 | £3,900 |
| Full Howden Ward | +20 (to ~150) | £600 | 150 | £4,500 / year |

**Total annual cost once fully scaled in Howden Ward ≈ £4,500** (planning estimate).
