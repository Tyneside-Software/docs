# How we create jobs in Tyneside

> The **jobs engine** — how Tyneside Software turns website work into junior engineering careers.  
> On the web: [tyneside.software](https://tyneside.software) (§ How we create jobs in Tyneside).

## Summary

While we’re building the core **logistics and work-management software**, our team will also build **professional websites for local businesses**.

Every website we create for a **paying customer** directly funds **real software engineering jobs** — starting at the very bottom.

## How it works

| Piece | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Core product** | Field-service / logistics software (scheduling, routing, portals, etc.) |
| **Parallel work** | Professional websites for local paying customers |
| **Who we hire** | People just **entering** the workforce — or **re-entering** it |
| **How we train** | From the ground up, on **real client work** |
| **Money flow** | Every pound of website revenue is recycled into **more junior roles** |

We deliberately take on new people at the start of their career path. We train them, put them on real delivery, and use commercial website work to **pay for the next junior role**.

## Living proof

Our own websites are built **in-house**:

- [tyneside.software](https://tyneside.software)  
- [tyneside.charity](https://tyneside.charity)  
- Cleaner portal / [tyneside.cleaning](https://tyneside.cleaning)  

They are both brand presence and **portfolio** — living proof of what we can deliver.

## The jobs engine

We’re not just writing software. We’re creating a pathway that turns **local talent** into **skilled software engineers** — one website and one junior role at a time.
